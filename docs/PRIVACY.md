# Privacy Policy for Mindwtr

**Effective Date:** February 2, 2026

Mindwtr ("we", "our", or "us") is designed as a local-first application. We respect your privacy and do not collect, store, or share your personal data.

**1. Data Storage**
All data created within the app (tasks, projects, notes) is stored locally on your device.

**2. Data Sync**
If you choose to use sync features (e.g., WebDAV, iCloud), your data is transmitted directly between your device and your chosen storage provider. We (the developer) do not have access to this data.

**3. Third-Party Services (AI)**
If you use the optional AI features, data is sent directly to the AI provider (e.g., OpenAI) using your personal API key. We do not act as a middleman and do not collect this data.

**4. Contact Us**
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository: https://github.com/dongdongbh/Mindwtr
